  would be:

\begin{align*}

    Speedup_{asymmetric}(f, n, r) &= \frac{1}{\frac{1-f}{perf(r)} + \frac{f}{pref(r) + n - r}} \\
    Speedup_{asymmetric}(0.9, n, r) &= \frac{1}{\frac{0.1}{pref(r)} + Speedup_{symmetric}(0.9, n-r, r)} \\
                       &= \frac{1}{\frac{0.1}{pref(r)} + \frac{1}{\frac{0.1 r}{(3 - r) pref(r)}}}
\end{align*}
